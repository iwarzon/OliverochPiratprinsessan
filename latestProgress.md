# Latest Progress — Oliver och Piratprinsessan

**Last updated:** 2026-06-24

## Current State

The project is a personal children's book — "Oliver och Piratprinsessan" — written by Robin Iwarzon for his daughters. It tells the story of a boy named Oliver who sets out on an adventure to find his missing parents, encountering pirates, princesses, and mystery along the way.

### Completed

- **Story**: 9 chapters, ~25 pages, written personally by Robin with AI assistance for proofreading
- **Illustrations**: 28 final page illustrations (numbered 0–27) plus cover (omslag), generated via ChatGPT with character design boards for consistency
- **Character design templates**: Oliver, Piratprinsessan, Generalsjostjarna, Linnea, Oliver's mamma, Oliver's pappa, sailors, Piratprinsessan's ship, Oliver's pappa's boat, Oliver's room
- **Audio recording**: Robin recorded himself reading the book chapter by chapter
- **Print submission**: Sent to Amazon Kindle for verification
- **"The Making Of" document**: Written (docx in repo root)

### In Progress / Next Steps

- **GitHub repo**: Initial commit and push of all assets (this session)
- **Digital reading platform**: Book should be free to read digitally online — needs a web-based reader
- **Open source licensing**: Decide and add a creative commons or similar license
- **Audio integration**: Potentially integrate recorded audio with the digital reader
