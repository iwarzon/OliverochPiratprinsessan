# Oliver och Piratprinsessan

A children's book about a boy named Oliver who sets out on a big adventure to find his missing parents — and along the way meets pirates, a princess, and more mystery than he bargained for.

This book was written by a dad for his daughters, and we're sharing it here so anyone can read it, enjoy it, and build on it.

---

## The story behind the story

This started as a bedtime story. My uncle used to tell made-up adventure stories when he babysat me and my sister, and it stuck with me. When my oldest daughter turned four, I started doing the same thing — making up stories about a boy called Oliver.

She loved it. But she also had an incredible memory, and any time I changed a detail between tellings, she called me out. So I started writing it down.

Over the course of a Swedish winter and spring, the story grew into nine chapters. My daughter became my toughest producer. Friends and family read early drafts. One colleague recorded himself reading it to his own daughter — her joy at hearing the story was one of the biggest motivators to keep going.

The illustrations were created with AI (ChatGPT), guided by hand-drawn character boards to keep things consistent across pages. The text is mine. The whole thing was put together during parental leave, in sleeping breaks and odd evenings, with a lot of help from technology and a lot more help from a five-year-old.

You can read the full behind-the-scenes story in [The making of Oliver and Piratprinsessan.docx](The%20making%20of%20Oliver%20and%20Piratprinsessan.docx).

## How to use this

**Read it** — Browse the illustrations in `Bilder/Final/` (numbered by page order, starting from 0). The cover is `omslag.png`.

**Print it** — A print version is in the works via Amazon Kindle. Details will be added here when available.

**Build on it** — Want to translate it, illustrate your own version, create an audiobook, build a reading app, or remix the story? Go for it. That's why it's here. Just give credit and keep your version open too (see license below).
